The NEXUS Pro Mechanical Keyboard represents the pinnacle of keyboard engineering. Built with a CNC-machined aluminum chassis and featuring our premium hot-swappable PCB, this keyboard is designed for enthusiasts who refuse to compromise.

Tri-mode connectivity lets you switch seamlessly between USB-C wired, 2.4GHz wireless, and Bluetooth 5.0 connections. The 4000mAh battery provides up to 200 hours of use on a single charge.

Each keyboard includes premium PBT double-shot keycaps, a braided USB-C cable, switch lubricant, and a premium carrying case.
